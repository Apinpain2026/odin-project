Classic Tomato Pasta

Description

This classic tomato pasta is a quick, delicious meal made with fresh tomatoes, garlic, and basil. Perfect for busy weeknights!

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 4 ripe tomatoes, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Grated Parmesan cheese (optional)

Steps

  1. Boil pasta according to package instructions.
  2. Heat olive oil in a pan and saut√© garlic until golden.
  3. Add tomatoes and cook for 10 minutes until soft.
  4. Season with salt and pepper, add basil leaves.
  5. Drain pasta and mix with the sauce.
  6. Serve hot with Parmesan cheese if desired.
