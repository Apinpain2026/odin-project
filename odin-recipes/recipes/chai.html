Masala Chai

Description

Masala chai is a spiced Indian tea made by brewing black tea with aromatic spices and herbs. It's warming, comforting, and full of flavor.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups water
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 teaspoons black tea leaves or tea bags
  • 2 cardamom pods
  • 1 small cinnamon stick
  • 2 cloves
  • 1/2 inch piece of ginger, grated
  • Sugar to taste

Steps

  1. Boil water with all the spices and ginger for 5 minutes.
  2. Add tea leaves and boil for another 2 minutes.
  3. Add milk and sugar, bring to a boil again.

  5. Serve hot and enjoy!
